Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to apply?

Requirements vary by position, but generally you need to be an active member of our community, have a good understanding of roleplay principles, and meet the minimum age requirement of 16 years.

How long does the application process take?

The application review process typically takes 3-7 days, depending on the volume of applications and the position you're applying for.

Can I apply for multiple positions?

Yes, you can apply for multiple positions, but we recommend focusing on one role at a time to increase your chances of success.

What happens if my application is rejected?

If your application is rejected, you'll receive feedback on areas for improvement. You can reapply after 30 days with a new application addressing those areas.
