FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need to own GTA V to play on your server?

Yes, you need to own a legitimate copy of Grand Theft Auto V on PC to play on our FiveM server.

Is there an age requirement to join?

Yes, you must be at least 16 years old to join our community and server.

Do I need a microphone to play?

Yes, a working microphone is required for proper roleplay communication. Text-based RP is allowed in certain situations, but voice communication is preferred.

Is there an application process?

Basic access to the server doesn't require an application. However, certain roles like police, EMS, or specific criminal organizations do require an application and interview process.

What if I'm new to roleplay?

We welcome newcomers! We have a helpful community and staff who can guide you through the basics of roleplay. We also have a mentorship program for new players.

Are there any paid features or VIP options?

We offer optional VIP packages that provide cosmetic items and quality-of-life improvements. However, we maintain a strict no-pay-to-win policy, ensuring fair gameplay for everyone.

COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

At Legion Roleplay, we believe in creating a positive and inclusive environment for all players. Here are some key guidelines to follow:

  • Treat all players with respect, regardless of background or experience level
  • Stay in character during roleplay interactions
  • Avoid metagaming (using out-of-character information in-game)
  • Report bugs and exploits rather than abusing them
  • Follow staff instructions and decisions
  • Use appropriate language in all communication channels

For a complete list of rules and guidelines, please visit our Rules Page.
